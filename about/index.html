After graduating from Olds College’s Horticulture program in 2019 I started landscaping out in Vancouver where I developed a broad range of experience; basic maintenance to full renovations. While there I also volunteered with Vancouver’s Stanley Park Ecology Society removing invasive species and providing public education about the park.

Returning to Calgary I held my first arborist job as part of the city’s urban forestry division. A couple years later I incorporated my own business and am now serving the surrounding Calgary area, primarily focused on the small towns nearest the foothills but am willing drive out east into the plains, or west into the mountains.
